About Me

I recently completed my studies in the Faculty of Law at Lund University. My unique strengths, setting me apart from most jurists, are comprehensive knowledge of programming and computers in general. I also find it easy to acquire knowledge in new areas, as exemplified by my having spent two year in the Bachelor's Program of Theoretical Physics where I received good grades (transcript provided upon request) and took a significant number of advanced courses in mathematics, physics, and programming. I also learned to program in R by myself in order to do a statistical analysis for my master's thesis. My ability in language combined with my understanding for both society, technology and nature makes me especially qualified for work where these fields intersect. One example is work dealing with digital rights – something that requires understanding for both law and technology.
